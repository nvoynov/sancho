Sancho Story

2023-01-28

Exercising with Punch and inspired by the result I decided to propose my private “clean domain development” services. Having written some content drafts, my first thought was to just use Jekyll as it is on nvoynov.github.io, but have searched a bit through the Internet I bumped into zenweb and decided to give it a try.

Fortunately, something went wrong :) and a few of my content pages, despite all my dancing with a tambourine, just failed to translate into HTML. Being looking through zenweb code I realized that it’s actually easy to design on my own.

So meet the Sancho :)
